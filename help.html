How can we help?


Increase energy efficiency in your home!


  • Keep the air conditioning a degree warmer than you usually would keep it
  • Turn off the lights when you leave a room
  • Invest in more efficient windows
  • Consider the switch to solar panels
  • Change your light bulbs to compact fluorescent lights (CFLs)

Make changes in your lifestyle!


  • Drive an electric car to eliminate dangerous fumes
  • Opt for more local vacations as opposed to ones that require you to get on a plane
  • If possible, bike or walk to work and the grocery store!
  • Use a reusable water bottle instead of using new plastic water bottles every day
  • Find out how to recycle (properly) by visiting the website of your local recycling service
  • Carpool with your friends when you’re going to the same place
  • Move to the city (suburbs are very unfriendly to the environment)
  • Don’t leave the water running while brushing your teeth!
  • Try making showering an in-and-out activity

Help combat deforestation!

  • Plant trees in your yard and neighborhood
  • Avoid palm products
  • Conserve paper

Advocate in your local and global community!


  • Sign the petitions of the World Wildlife Fund
  • Create and participate in environmental advocacy and awareness clubs
  • Write letters to leadership
